## Summary

Applied two design system alignment corrections to `frontend/src/components/Chip.tsx` based on Aria's design language review.

## Corrections Applied

### 1. Border Radius
- **Before:** `rounded-full` (pill shape, 9999px)
- **After:** `rounded` (matches `--bevel-radius: 10px`)
- **Reason:** App's visual language uses soft-rounded (10px), not full-pill shapes for status indicators

### 2. Padding
- **Before:** `px-2 py-0.5` (8px / 2px)
- **After:** `px-2.5 py-1` (10px / 4px)
- **Reason:** Previous padding was too tight, causing text to crowd against chip edges

### 3. Backdrop Blur (Removed)
- **Removal:** `backdrop-blur-sm`
- **Reason:** No-op inside frosted containers; effectively invisible
